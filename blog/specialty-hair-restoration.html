Specialty Hair Restoration: Facial Grafts & No-Shave Techniques

📅 July 20, 2026 ✍️ Written by: Dr. Asif Ameer
Home > Blog > Specialty Hair Resto...

"Hair restoration is no longer limited to treating male pattern baldness. Micro-surgical advancements now allow for successful reconstruction of beards, mustaches, and eyebrows, along with specialized no-shave transplants designed specifically for women."

1. Beard and Mustache Reconstruction

Facial hair transplants require meticulous design. Facial hair grows at much flatter angles compared to scalp hair (10 to 20 degrees) and exits the skin in unique directions across different parts of the face.

We harvest single-hair follicular units from the back of the scalp and implant them at a flat angle to avoid bristly growth, restoring density and natural definition to patchy beards and mustaches.

2. Eyebrow Micrografting

Reconstructing eyebrows demands the highest level of artistic accuracy. We harvest fine, single-hair follicles from the back of the neck or behind the ears. Using DHI Choi pens with needle sizes of 0.6mm, we implant each graft at flat angles to match your natural eyebrow growth pattern and restore symmetrical brow definition.

Written by: Dr. Asif Ameer

Dr. Asif Ameer is the Medical Director and Clinical Lead at HT Hair Transplant Clinic. With over a decade of clinical experience, he has successfully performed more than 5,000 surgeries, specializing in advanced FUE and DHI restoration.
