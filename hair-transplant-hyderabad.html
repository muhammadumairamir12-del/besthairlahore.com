Our Specialized Clinical Treatments

State-of-the-art procedures customized for your hair patterns.

👨‍⚕️

Micro-FUE Transplant

Follicular Unit Extraction utilizing tiny punches (0.75mm - 0.85mm) to harvest grafts without leaving linear scars. Perfect for broad bald areas.

✒️

DHI (Choi Pen) Implantation

Direct Hair Implantation offering precise depth and growth angle control. Ideal for hairline lowering and high-density crown fills.

🧔

Beard & Mustache Transplant

Facial micrografting designed for sparse cheeks or patchy goatees, placing grafts at flat growth angles for a natural beard.

💉

PRP Hair Therapy

High-concentration growth factor therapy derived from your own blood, spun via a double centrifuge to thicken thinning hair shafts.
