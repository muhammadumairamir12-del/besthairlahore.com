Why Graft Calculations Matter

Over-harvesting the donor zone is irreversible. Safe planning ensures lifetime results.

Protecting the Safe Donor Area

Grafts are harvested from the back and sides of the head. Since this hair is limited, harvesting too many grafts in one go can cause visible thinning (moth-eaten look) in your donor zone. A precise calculation ensures we stay within safe extraction limits.

FUE vs DHI Placement Densities

Standard FUE is perfect for large areas, yielding 35-45 grafts per square centimeter. DHI (Choi Pen technique) allows closer placement, yielding up to 55-65 grafts per square centimeter, making it highly effective for rebuilding hairlines or filling thin spots without shaving.
