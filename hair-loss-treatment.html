Medical Hair Loss Diagnostics & Treatments

Home > Services > Hair Loss Treatment

Hair loss is a complex medical condition influenced by genetics, hormones, nutrition, and lifestyle. Before considering surgery, it is important to address the root causes of hair loss. We provide comprehensive non-surgical hair loss management, combining medical treatments, topicals, and diagnostics.

Diagnostic Trichoscopy Mapping

We begin every hair loss consultation with a detailed trichoscopy assessment. By examining your scalp under high magnification, we analyze follicle density, hair shaft diameter, and localized scalp health.

Based on your assessment, we compile a personalized medical treatment plan. This may combine FDA-approved DHT blockers, custom topical solutions, and regenerative treatments (such as exosome therapy) to stabilize hair fall and thicken thinning areas.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about this treatment.

Male hair thinning is typically caused by Androgenetic Alopecia (male pattern baldness), which is triggered by a sensitivity to DHT, a byproduct of testosterone.

Medical treatments are highly effective at stabilizing hair loss and thickening thinning hairs. For completely bald zones, surgical restoration is usually required.
