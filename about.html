Our Story & Mission

HT Hair Transplant was founded with a singular mission — to restore not just hair, but the confidence that comes with it. Based in DHA, Lahore, our clinic brings internationally trained surgeons and the latest medical technology to Pakistan's most discerning patients.

We specialize in advanced FUE and DHI hair transplant techniques, combined with a full spectrum of aesthetic treatments including Exosome Therapy, PRP, Botox, Fillers, and Laser services. Every treatment plan is personalized to the individual patient's anatomy, goals, and budget.

Our commitment is simple: deliver natural-looking, permanent results with absolute transparency. No hidden charges. No unrealistic promises. Just honest medicine, expert hands, and life-changing outcomes.

Dr. Asif Ameer

Senior Hair Transplant Surgeon

Dr. Asif Ameer is a highly respected, internationally trained hair restoration surgeon with over a decade of clinical experience. Specializing in advanced follicular unit extraction (FUE) and direct hair implantation (DHI), he has successfully performed over 5,000 surgeries, helping patients achieve natural hairline reconstructions.

He is an active member of international restoration societies, dedicated to keeping HT Hair Transplant at the forefront of global clinical standards. Every hair design is personally drafted and supervised by him to guarantee maximum graft survival and cosmetic symmetry.
