Our Story & Mission

HT Aesthetiq was founded with a singular mission — to restore not just hair, but the confidence that comes with it. Based in DHA, Lahore, our clinic brings internationally trained surgeons and the latest medical technology to Pakistan's most discerning patients.

We specialize in advanced FUE and DHI hair transplant techniques, combined with a full spectrum of aesthetic treatments including Exosome Therapy, PRP, Botox, Fillers, and Laser services. Every treatment plan is personalized to the individual patient's anatomy, goals, and budget.

Our commitment is simple: deliver natural-looking, permanent results with absolute transparency. No hidden charges. No unrealistic promises. Just honest medicine, expert hands, and life-changing outcomes.
