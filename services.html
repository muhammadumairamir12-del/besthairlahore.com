FUE Hair Transplant

FUE (Follicular Unit Extraction) is the most advanced form of surgical hair restoration. Individual follicular units are extracted one by one from the donor area using a specialized micro-punch tool and implanted into the recipient zone. The result is a completely natural hairline with no linear scar.

This minimally invasive technique is performed under local anesthesia, ensuring a comfortable experience. The micro-punches leave tiny dots that heal within days. Patients can return to non-strenuous activities within 3–5 days.

Our surgeons use the latest motorized extraction tools combined with custom implanter pens, allowing us to achieve high density with precise angle control that mimics the natural growth direction.

  • ✓ Permanent natural results
  • ✓ No linear scarring
  • ✓ Local anesthesia, minimal discomfort
  • ✓ Customized hairline design
  • ✓ Suitable for men and women

DHI Hair Transplant

DHI (Direct Hair Implantation) represents the next evolution in follicular transplantation. Using a specialized Choi implanter pen, grafts are inserted directly into the scalp without the need to pre-create recipient channels. This reduces handling time and maximizes graft survival rates.

The Choi pen provides surgeons with unprecedented control over implantation depth, angle, and direction. This results in more natural-looking density and faster patient recovery compared to traditional methods.

DHI is particularly effective for patients who require high-density coverage in smaller areas or those who wish to avoid shaving the recipient zone entirely.

  • ✓ Higher graft survival rate
  • ✓ Faster healing
  • ✓ No pre-made incisions
  • ✓ Maximum density control
  • ✓ Minimal bleeding

Exosome Therapy

Exosome therapy is a breakthrough regenerative treatment that harnesses the power of extracellular vesicles to stimulate dormant hair follicles. Exosomes carry growth factors, proteins, and RNA that signal follicles to re-enter the active growth phase.

This non-surgical treatment is ideal for patients experiencing early-stage hair thinning or those who wish to enhance the results of a hair transplant procedure. It can be used as a standalone treatment or combined with PRP.

Sessions are quick, typically lasting 30–45 minutes, with zero downtime. Most patients notice improvements in hair density and thickness within 3–6 months of starting treatment.

  • ✓ Non-surgical, zero downtime
  • ✓ Stimulates dormant follicles
  • ✓ Enhances transplant outcomes
  • ✓ Quick 30–45 min sessions
  • ✓ Safe for all hair types

Hair PRP Treatment

Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP) therapy uses your own blood's concentrated growth factors to stimulate hair follicle activity and strengthen existing hair. A small blood sample is drawn, centrifuged to concentrate platelets, then injected into the scalp.

PRP contains over 20 growth factors including PDGF, VEGF, and TGF-β, all of which play crucial roles in the hair growth cycle. Clinical studies have shown significant improvement in hair density, thickness, and tensile strength with consistent treatment.

A standard PRP protocol involves 3–4 monthly sessions followed by quarterly maintenance. The entire procedure takes under one hour and requires no recovery time.

  • ✓ 100% natural — uses your own blood
  • ✓ Strengthens existing hair
  • ✓ No chemicals or drugs
  • ✓ Zero downtime
  • ✓ Clinically proven efficacy

Acne & Scars Treatment

Our advanced acne and scar treatment programs combine multiple modalities — including fractional laser resurfacing, chemical peels, and microneedling — to deliver dramatic improvements in skin clarity and texture.

Active acne is addressed through targeted laser energy that destroys acne-causing bacteria and reduces sebaceous gland activity. Post-acne pigmentation and scarring are treated with resurfacing techniques that stimulate collagen remodeling.

We offer customized protocols based on your skin type, acne severity, and scarring pattern, ensuring optimal results with minimal risk. Most patients require 4–6 sessions spaced 3–4 weeks apart.

  • ✓ Fades dark spots and PIH
  • ✓ Improves skin texture
  • ✓ Stimulates collagen production

Botox & Fillers

Our aesthetic medicine team administers FDA-approved botulinum toxin (Botox) and hyaluronic acid dermal fillers to address signs of aging with precision and artistry. Treatments are customized to achieve natural-looking rejuvenation.

Botox relaxes dynamic wrinkles — forehead lines, crow's feet, frown lines — by temporarily inhibiting muscle movement. Results appear within 3–7 days and last 4–6 months. Dermal fillers restore lost volume in cheeks, lips, and under-eye areas.

All procedures are performed by trained medical professionals in a clinical setting using sterile, CE-marked products. We prioritize a natural aesthetic that enhances your features without looking overdone.

Laser Hair Removal

Our medical-grade laser hair removal system delivers permanent hair reduction by targeting melanin in hair follicles with precise laser energy. The follicle is destroyed at the root, preventing future regrowth without damaging surrounding skin.

Treatment is fast and comfortable — full legs can be treated in under 45 minutes. A built-in cooling system protects the skin during treatment. Multiple sessions are required as only active (anagen) follicles are affected per session.

We use advanced diode and Nd:YAG laser systems suitable for all Fitzpatrick skin types, including darker South Asian skin tones common in Pakistan. A typical course involves 6–8 sessions spaced 4–6 weeks apart.

  • ✓ Permanent hair reduction
  • ✓ Eliminates ingrown hairs
  • ✓ Safe for South Asian skin
  • ✓ Fast treatment sessions
  • ✓ Cost-effective versus lifelong waxing
